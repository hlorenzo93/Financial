This program is for people who wants to control their expense with food, gas, goods, shopping, etc...

In this program it will be able to organized the expense by type.

You enter the amount and then add up in total.

Introduce a calendar were the user can see the total amount expense per day, month and year.

What will be the first activity? Graphic percentage of all the expense per type.

Button at the right bottom corner, to start the second activity.

In the second activity, theirs a textfield editable to enter new amount of expense per type.
